EscortMe
Have you or your friend ever faced a dangerous situation when you've been coming back home? Have you ever heard about missing people? Have you ever needed to call emergency services as quick as possible? If you answered "yes" on at least on of that questions, this application is surely for you.
Whether you're shopping for a new outfit, camping in the great outdoors or enroute to a party, the EscortME GPS functionality makes it easy to locate your friends in real-time.
HOW IT WORKS
EscortMe is an application that is capable of:

  • analyzing your location and safety status, storing it on cloud in real time

  • notifying other user (in user-created group and any person nearby) about your current location and potential dangerous situations

  • generating statistics related to your way back home

  • configuring Emergency Button - configurations of buttons that trigger safety alarm

  • safety alarm - starts to stream your voice, accelerometer data, and localization, notifying your friends (and any person that could be exposed to danger) about your situation

  • calling security services with just one click

  • receiving push notifications about surrounding dangerous events making it easy to find a better, safe way to your destination
  • detecting abnormal behaviors - falling down, fighting, shouting, running away, quick change of a way of transport
